Account

An account on ApiRTC platform consists in a main Enterprise and and admin User.

One apiKey is associated to one Enterprise. The apiKey is a simple string that identifies a client application without any principal. It is useful for accessing the api, associating ApiRTC usage with your project for quota and billing.

ApiRTC allows to create children Enterprises to the main Enterprise, each one having it's own apiKey. This is usefull to isolate quota and billing of different projects.

Users and participants

Depending on projects and needs, communications participants may be:

  • Users managed by ApiRTC platform,
  • users managed externaly,
  • guests

ApiRTC managed Users are associated to an Enterprise.

Conversation

A Conversation happens between participants. They may or may not be Users defined on the ApiRTC platform.

Usage based billing

ApiRTC billing is based on usage.

Video Conversation billing is based on the number of minutes in communication and per participant.
